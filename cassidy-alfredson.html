Cassidy Alfredson

My art is a documentation of everything I am and will be. It is a collage of my own memories but also the stories of those I encounter. It explores themes of womanhood, the loss of innocence, love, spirituality, queerness, and race. Often jumping between the view of storyteller and listener, the divine and loyal subject, and of the watched and the watcher. My work is deeply influenced by my spirituality and background and is the source from which my art is created. In its true essence, it is a homage to those who came before me and a reminder to those who will soon come after me.
